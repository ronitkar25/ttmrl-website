Located in the Starzl Biomedical Science Tower, our lab sits at the center of biomedical innovation and clinical excellence.

The Trauma and Transfusion Medicine Research Laboratory (TTMRL) welcomes inquiries from highly motivated researchers and collaborators. Our lab is focused on understanding platelet biology and leveraging genomic tools to investigate mechanisms of injury, inflammation, and organ failure. We aim to translate this knowledge into novel therapeutic strategies that improve outcomes for critically ill patients.
